About Us

We Take Care of Your Healthy Life

Founded in 1997 by a team of India’s leading cardiologists, Medicos Hospitals started its journey as a 100-bedded Heart Institute with a core team of 20 cardiologists, 1 operating theatre, and 1 catheterization laboratory. 25 years later, the Medicos Hospitals Group is a multi-speciality healthcare provider with 17 healthcare facilities serving 7 cities across 6 states in India. It is the regional leader in South and Central India and is amongst the top 5 Pan-Indian hospital chains. Medicos Hospitals delivers comprehensive care in over 30 specialities. Adopting a service-oriented delivery model,Medicos Hospitals provides cost-effective medical care, with an uncompromising commitment to its core purpose – ‘To make quality healthcare accessible at affordable costs to all'
